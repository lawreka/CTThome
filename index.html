CONVERGING TECHNOLOGICAL TRENDS
TEAM RED
Each module in this course was approximately one sprint long. They explore emerging technologies, which can only exist at the point in time that two or more sufficiently advanced technolgies converge. Team Red is Giacomo Flaim (CD), Kathryn Lawrence (CD), Katherine Rakitskaya (J), Felix von Stutterheim (J), and Yesim Süküt (BP)

module 1: Mind Music

Mind Music explores the convergence of EEG technology and electronic music, culminating in a project sponsored by Smirnoff allowing computer-generated music to be produced by brain wave control

module 2: VR Porn

Our VR Porn exhibition takes place in a WebVR simulation powered by AFrame. Recommended for viewing on Mozilla Firefox desktop browser, or any mobile browser and Google Cardboard.

module 3: Chat Bots

Read a timeline of chatbot history illustrating the explosion of chatbots by the convergence of growing messaging apps and developments in AI, featuring long form research about AI psychology and other topics.
